Love

Is it unconditional , unintentional unapolegetic .... This love ??
Or
A dainty dent adorning a plain wall just like an alcove ....
And it wraps you unaware , not sudden though ,
A serene surreal mist surrounds the soul , you know !!!!
Is it just a pleasant warmth that engulfs in its embrace ?? Indigenous imprint lasting for a lifetime trace !!!
However sombre it may sound -
we fail to understand thus ,
Compelled by a longing heart ,
A nest is built in places not meant for us ..
- Afaaf
