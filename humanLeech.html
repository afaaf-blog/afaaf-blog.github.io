HUMAN or LEECH

The human race is at risk of being extinct ,
Quite alarming for us , I think .....
And No ,
I don't mean the homosapiens will vanish ...
But the humble humans sans humanity don't exist !!!!
As far as one sees .....
you don't have a choice... As if it matters ,
The unthoughtful , the uncaring , the unmindful has brought humanity to tatters .....
Selfish Soulless scavengers rule the roost ,
Making moolah at the cost of everything is on a boost !!!!
What has happened to the ethics of coexistence ??
Living ,
Nope .... Outliving others has become life's essence ...
Mortals has MORALS intertwined or s o I thought ,
The battle between Me versus Us - constantly being fought !!!
Tell me , pray , dare I beseech .....
How will you like to be addressed ???
Human or leech ........
