Out of the Box

There's world too outside my four walls , By no means I'd confine...
A peep from my window shows me the tell tale signs

Out there, is bare brilliant brightness,
N vibrance to colour my soul...
The world, the universe beckons me to step out
N reach my goal!!!
Ohh, n I do have delicate dahlias to decorate,
to nurture, to beautify the fate!!

Ummmm...
You should have told me about the unruly wind n the harsh sun,
I held out my flowers to wane the scorch but
Alas!!
lost their innocence...

- Afaaf
