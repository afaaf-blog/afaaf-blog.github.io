The Closet Woes

Heard a muffled cry while idling before the idiot box ,
My closet beckons..
Being a good samaritan , I uncovered the Pandora Box !!!!
The unruly heap welcomed me with a sudden fall ,
Finding my way out of the maze was an order tall !!!
Shoving them aside , I sat in the middle of maddening mess ,
This cleanliness drive is surely adding to my stress !!!
Now now , known for my sartorial sense ...
I need to have options plenty !!
Shouldn't I have all the colours of the rainbow to adorn me daintly ??? üíÉüèª

The golden rule is to pair the shoes and a lovely handbag to catch ....

sneakers n flats -the comfy shoes if you may !!!!
How do I dispose off the apparels so lovingly bought, collected over the years ,
Each one has a memory fondly to share !!!

must be a genius minus doubt ,
