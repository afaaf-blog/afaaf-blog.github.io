Knock

Did I hear a knock ??
The door- closed for so long...
That I do not remember...
So was I in a deep slumber??
Or was it me who bolted it tight,
Crossing the threshold didn't seem right..
Now I see a shiny streak,
Uninvited, unannounced barging in..
Is it trying to sneak???
The deep dark abyss has been my recluse,
Guarding my secrets, apprehensions that I choose...
But who could be this intruder??
A missed opportunity, or fate beckoning?
May it's time now to claim the rightful meaning...
To step out and savour the light,
To bask in its glory n
Seek the sight!!!
No I am not leaving behind my companion , my recluse...
The darkness follows me in light, none to refuse!!!

- Afaaf
