The Twin Flame

What does anybody aspire for in life -
self esteem to hold head high and money to comfort...
Affection of dear ones...These should keep me covered...
What is it that I still crave for???
not able to put my finger on...
Someone who listens to my endless prattle..
Or even hears me when the mute is on!!!
My twin flame...Just like me who can enjoy the soulful silence serene..
A glance just into my eyes n know how I have been...
My hands are full, you know,
And there is so much work to accomplish,
Yet a missing piece of a jigsaw is all I earnestly wish!!!
Willing to embrace me with all my lacunae...
Accept me as I am..
As a friend, companion or a bae...
Should I look out for this mate outside
or first explore in???
Coz unless I don't come to terms to who I am,
My Odyssey - the search for a soulmate cannot begin!!!
Hey you, out there...
will have to wait for me to arrive,
I will settle for nothing less than the best, I so longingly strive!!!
- Afaaf
