Wait...

When the Fall comes ,
I eagerly wait for both of you ,
To sit and chat for a little while ,
Setting afloat giggles a few !!
Though you talk in whispers ,
I hear it all !!!
If i had a face , it would have have a smile all along ...
But i also sit to witness the lovely young couple ,
The cute cuddles and endearing embrace ,
Away from the prying eyes ,
Out in woods , they find solace !!!!!
And then,
this couple of bygone era ,
Who seek refuge ..
When the fatigue overtakes ,
Head resting on his shoulders ,
Her shy smile amidst wrinkles
saves the grace !!!!
It got to be the fall colours and the ochre hue ,
Enchants them to a pretty world
Stealing lovely moments a few !!
When winters come
And the trees stand bare ,
Lonely n deserted ,
I'll still be waiting there !!!!!
