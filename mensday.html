Men's Day

Fables say , we are born from their ribs ..
The same flesh n blood makes us kin too..
Epitome of strength and valour ,
Courage , chivalry - virtues to name a few !!
Ohh !! N What do we do without them ???
After all , God cannot be blamed everytime for the mayhem !!🤪🤪🤪🤪
How can we undermine the roles they play so adept ??
Like ,
When a heavy table needs to be shifted or attic things needs to be kept ?????
And , what do we do when a lizard or a roach pays a visit 😊😊,
Screaming alone doesn't shoo them away, isn't it 🤭??
A strong brave man is, then, needed to save the damsels in distress ...
So dear ladies , men are indispensable ,
Trust you me ,
M in no mood to digress 🤣 !!!!
- Afaaf
