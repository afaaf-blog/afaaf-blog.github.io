Broken

He left her with a broken heart ,
Wobbling, wounded , writhing in pain .....
For him ... An end to another story .....
Shattered , rejected , what did she gain ??
The cracks would accede to the torturous torment,
or so he thought !!!!!
Lost in the cacophony ,
Wounds left ajar , agony sought .....
Much to his chargin ,
She rose to conquer ,
The fissures in her heart ,
Allowed the light to enter !!!
The scars still exist to decorate her soul and heart,
She , a lot wiser , a great deal stronger ....
Propelled to a new start !!!!!!!
