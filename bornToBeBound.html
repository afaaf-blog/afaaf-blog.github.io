Born to be bound???

Limitations -
Part and parcel our life...
Bonded or bounded??
Two perspectives majorly in strife!!!
Do love and affection, bound you
Or do they set you free???
Adding fuel to your
Soaring,
Do they propel you to excel?
Or do these put your feet in shackles..?
"You are not safe beyond,
Better be within the confines!!"
Epics have enough mention,
scriptures too define...
Strangely, Evil was hovering loose.. she was the one who was bound..
Physically safe, but her psyche was left ajar for the hounds..
Always judged by her clothes or her time to return home,
Restrictions on how to walk, talk or laughing aloud,
I despise the discriminating demeanour, unashamedly allowed...
If 'man' was born free and claims to be in chain everywhere,
What worse can be said for women, need I dare??ðŸ˜”

Her confident smile and assuring stride will neversuffer any slack!!!
