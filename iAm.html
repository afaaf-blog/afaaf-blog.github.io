I Am Me

I don't aspire to be You
Or your copy or a mere shadow ....
I am me , basking, resonating in my high n low !!!
A shadow is bound to follow ,
Doesn't even has a shape of its own ,
Voiceless , devoid of any bright hues ....
Or coutours , not even a smile to evade the blues !!!
I wish , you realise soon,
we are not equal .....
Ohh , Don't get me wrong ,
Different but no less ,
Our souls sing a different song ....
We both want to reach high ,
A different zenith , a different sky !!
Still doesn't mean , our paths are separate,
Partners beyond the birth , united by fate !!!
I choose to accompany you till the end ,
Neither to follow , nor to lead ,
Our travelling tale will tend to be more terrific ,
When I partner , I contribute , I complement in all you do ,
To be specific !!!!!
- Afaaf
